    What is the Festival of the Lost?

    Festival of the Lost is a time for remembering those we've lost in the fight against the Darkness. This is not a sad time. No, this is a happy time to remember all the good those people brought. Eva Levante is the person to see to get the festivities kicked off. She will supply you with a mask and will send you to the Spider in the Tangled Shore. From there you'll want to run the Haunted Forest, a darkened version of the Infinite Forest, to collect Candy, Chocolate Strange Coins, and other loot. Whether you be a Franken-Titan, Were-Lock, or Vampire Hunter, the Festival of the Lost will provide you with hours of fun.
