    Paladins

    Looks up "How to start my Paladins review..."

    All jokes aside, this one's going to be really tough to make a full review on. Either you love Paladins or you don't, that's just how it is. I can guaruntee, though, if you like Overwatch, you'll love Paladins. First off, it's Free-to-Play, and it's on multiple platforms, just like Overwatch. Although Overwatch is also on multiple platforms, the developers, Hi-Rez Studios, beat Blizzard to the Nintendo Switch punch. That gave them the marketing advantage for a while, then Blizzard came in and stole the money right from under Hi-Rez's nose.

    That's enough with video game politics, now into the actual review. 😁

    Actual Review

    As I said above, either you love or hate Paladins. It's been nice to me so I, personally, am leaning more toward the 'love it' side of the game, so my opinion may very well be biased. First off, the game isn't pay-to-play meaning everything you could possibly pay real-world money for is all cosmetic so there is absolutely no competitive advantage. Yes, you could pay $9.99 to get a champion or 2, or you could do the actual grind, get better at the game, join a team, and make some cash. See, for all you pay-to-play-ers, there's a little something called grinding... nevermind that, I'm going to stop venting and actually review now. When you actually get good at a game, it becomes muscle memory, how people play, what the competitive metas are, little stuff like that can go a long way in how enjoyable your time playing Paladins can be. If you can't manage to keep up with all those things, then this game isn't for you.

    I'm giving this game a lower rating because it's such a competitive game and may not be for everyone. I know it wasn't for me at first.
