    Bloons TD Battles

    The game in a nutshell... Pop. Send. Repeat.

    Bloons TD Battles can get very tricky, depending on what mode you play, though it is very repetitive. Like it says above, the game practically is pop your bloons, send some bloons to your opponent for Eco, repeat. Also, it's supposed to be free-to-play but somme of the game is locked behind a paywall, which I just don't understand.

    Though thhis game can get repetitive, there are a few positive aspects to look out on. For one, it's a versus BTD game. Like, come on. Which Bloons player doesn't like that? Two, You can naturally unlock most of the stuff that's blocked behind microtransactions, such as the Cobra tower and Monkey Farmers. Robot Farmers on the other hand, yeah, those... Those are in-app purchase only. But there isn't a point to them if you've got as many Monkey farmers as one of my friends. He has over 3000!

    All in all, what I've got to say about this game adds up to this, if you are looking for a good Bloons game, don't play a Free-to-Play like BTD Battles or Bloons Adventure Time TD, pay the $10 for Bloons TD 6 where everything isn't locked behind microtransactions, because most stuff in Battles, is.

    For repetitiveness and lots of microtransactions. (If repetitiveness is even a word...)
