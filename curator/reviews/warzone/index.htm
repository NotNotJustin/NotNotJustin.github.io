    Call of Duty: Warzone

    Warzone... How do I put this one into words? Other than people hacking their way to victory, cheating their way to prototype weapons from their pass, or just finding yourself an overall sweat to play against, the game is mostly enjoyable. I personally do not own Modern Warfare, the game Warzone is based on, but that only limits which operators I can use. It never really limits gameplay, as operators are purely cosmetic. The game is free to play and that's what makes it unique. It's a free to play Call of Duty game. Other than Call of Duty Mobile, this is the only time that this has happened in COD's history.

    The real point that is going to be made throughout this review is, the game is extremely, extremely competitive. Even in the game's less competitive mode, Plunder, it is really fast paced, given there are a maximum 125 players in a match at a time, and loot is limited. Whether you pick up cash, or are on the hunt for prey, you're bound to be killed by a sniper or someone with Dead Silence and a supressed P90. Long story short, if you're going to play this game, do so after playing an aim trainer because if you don't, you're goin to have a hard time with realistic mechanics, truly sweaty players, and overall mentally straining gameplay.

    Yay for stress! Sarcasm at its finest.
